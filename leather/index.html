The Art of Leather

Generations of mastery in every stitch

Our leather is vegetable-tanned, hand-dyed, and finished by families who have guarded their techniques for over a century. No two pieces are identical — each carries the signature of the artisan who shaped it.

From full-grain buffalo hides sourced across India to precision hand-stitching with waxed threads, every step honours a tradition that predates the Taj itself.
