The Art of Inlay

Centuries of patience in every tessera

Our marble is hand-selected from the quarries of Makrana, then carved, polished, and inlaid with semi-precious stones using the same Parchinkari technique that adorned the Taj Mahal. Each flower, each vine, is a study in precision that spans weeks of silent labour.

From geometric medallions to flowing arabesques, every piece is a testament to the belief that beauty is measured not in hours, but in generations.
